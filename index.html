HOWL








First spend all your attribute points using the 'Raise ...' buttons.

If you have enough gold you can buy items from Goblin Dave's Used Goods Emporium, or you can save it up for later.

Higher tier items are stronger but cost more. Every time you win a fight you'll receive either an item or gold.

Equip items using the 'Sell/Use Items' button under 'Your Adventure:'.

When you're ready to fight choose an enemy you think you can beat and click the 'Fight a ...' button then 'Attack' (watch out for your special attacks too!).

Fighting uses up Energy and if you take damage you'll lose Health as well, you'll then receive 1 Energy and Health back each minute until you're at full points again.

If you lose all your Health you'll be revived but it will cost you 2 Energy.

Good Luck!
